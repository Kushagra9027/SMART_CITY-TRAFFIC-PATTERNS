# Week 02 Progress Report

## Project

Smart City Traffic Forecasting

## Duration

Week 02

## Objectives for Week 02

The primary objective for this week was to perform detailed Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the traffic dataset and understand traffic behavior across different junctions and time periods.

---

## Activities Completed

### 1. Dataset Exploration

* Loaded and examined the traffic forecasting dataset.
* Analyzed dataset dimensions, structure, and feature descriptions.
* Verified data quality and dataset consistency.
* Generated statistical summaries for traffic-related features.

### 2. Data Quality Assessment

* Performed missing value analysis.
* Verified completeness of the dataset.
* Confirmed data readiness for further analysis.

### 3. Date-Time Processing

* Converted date-time information into appropriate formats.
* Extracted temporal features including:

  * Year
  * Month
  * Day
  * Hour
  * Weekday

### 4. Traffic Pattern Analysis

* Examined traffic volume distributions.
* Investigated traffic behavior across multiple junctions.
* Analyzed variations in vehicle counts over time.
* Explored traffic trends using visualization techniques.

### 5. Junction-wise Analysis

* Compared traffic volumes across different junctions.
* Identified high-traffic and low-traffic locations.
* Investigated differences in traffic behavior between junctions.

### 6. Time-based Analysis

* Analyzed traffic variations across different hours of the day.
* Studied weekday traffic patterns.
* Investigated temporal traffic trends and peak activity periods.

### 7. Data Visualization

Generated visualizations for:

* Traffic trends over time
* Junction-wise traffic comparisons
* Hour-wise traffic analysis
* Weekday traffic analysis

## Key Observations

* Traffic volume varies significantly across different junctions.
* Distinct peak traffic periods can be observed during specific hours.
* Temporal patterns suggest that traffic forecasting models can leverage time-based features for prediction.
